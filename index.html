Settings

Adjust your game preferences and settings. These settings will only be applied to this browser.

Notification Location

Designates where the notifications for various events (such as exceptional crafts) will appear.

Battle Log Length

Set the maximum amount of lines to be displayed in the battle log for Adventures. Default value is 30, minimum accepted value of 5, maximum accepted value of 999.

Toggle HP & AP Bars in Combat Area

Toggle whether to display or hide the HP and AP bars in Combat Area during Adventures.

Reset All Settings

Reset your settings to default values or if your settings are corrupted. This will reload the game.
